About Us

At Ufarm, we are passionate about helping farmers succeed. Our platform connects farmers with the resources they need to grow and sell their produce to reliable buyers. Our mission is to create a more sustainable agricultural system, one that benefits both farmers and consumers.

We believe that technology can play a key role in achieving this mission. We are building a platform that empowers farmers to make better decisions and streamline their operations. We are committed to driving innovation in the agricultural sector and supporting the growth of rural communities.

Our Vision

To be the leading technology platform for sustainable agriculture, connecting farmers with the resources they need to thrive.

Our Mission

To empower farmers with the tools, knowledge, and resources they need to increase productivity, profitability, and sustainability.

Our Objectives

  • Support smallholder farmers in Massajja Division
  • Facilitate access to financing and insurance
  • Reduce post-harvest losses and food waste
  • Promote sustainable farming practices
  • Create value for all stakeholders
